Welcome to MineSweep, a minesweeper clone. The goal is to preferably isolate the locations of all of the mines in the field. Click a cell to test it. If there are any mines in its proximity, the quantity will appear. RIGHT-CLICK (with mouse) or TOUCH and HOLD to flag a particular cell. This will mark the spaces you believe have mines, and protect you if inadvertantly click/tap that cell. Between games, you'll have a chance to change the grid size/increase difficulty.
