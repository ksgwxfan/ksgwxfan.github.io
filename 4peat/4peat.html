My dad used to work extensively at Radio Shack, so we'd always have bunches of radio-shack products and toys. This is a program that is similar to the Pocket-Repeat (like Simon).

The sounds were made via Goldwave. I used a youtube video review of the Pocket Repeat to match similar pitch.
