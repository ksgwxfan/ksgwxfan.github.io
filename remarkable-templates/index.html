reMarkable Templates

The reMarkable Tablet is an e-ink device that supports taking notes on the screen. This page will feature various templates and PDF files formatted for the device. It will not contain instructions on how to transfer them to your device.
Basketball Court Templates

Included in this template pack is a PDF of various basketball-court diagram layouts, optimized for the rM tablet. In addition, PNG files are included, suitable as templates. Included court types are NBA, NCAA, FIBA, and High School. Ideas of use would be for a shot-chart or designing plays.
